Projects Trading Strategy Evaluation (Python)

This project evaluates stock trading strategies using three technical indicators: B%, PPO, and Stochastic Oscillator. Manual Strategy and Strategy Learner based on Random Forest classification are compared against a benchmark using in-sample and out-of-sample data. Results show that while both strategies outperform the benchmark in-sample, the Manual Strategy continues to perform well out-of-sample, whereas the Strategy Learner struggles with unseen data and is negatively impacted by trading costs.

Churn and Diabetes Prediction using KNN, ANN and SVM (Python)

This project compares their performances and examines the impact of data dimensionality and size. Results show that ANN performs best overall with moderate computational cost, SVM is effective but computationally expensive, and KNN struggles due to the curse of dimensionality, challenging the assumption that larger datasets always lead to better model performance.

Dimension Reduction and Clustering (Python)
